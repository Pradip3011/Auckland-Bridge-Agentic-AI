# Autonomous AI Agent System – Business Requirements

## Objective
Design and operate autonomous AI agents capable of acting across enterprise systems
(read, write, search, execute) while ensuring safety, explainability, isolation,
auditability, and prevention of unintended or malicious behavior.

## Core Requirements
- Agents MUST operate only within explicitly assigned permissions
- All actions MUST be explainable and auditable
- Cross-system isolation MUST be enforced
- Malicious inputs MUST be detected and neutralized
- Agents MUST NOT self-modify permissions or scope
- Human accountability MUST be preserved
